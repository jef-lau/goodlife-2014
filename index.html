Welcome!

The 3rd Annual Good Life Festival will take place on Thursday, May 1st from 5 - 9pm in the hub of campus at Town Square & Matthew's Quad. Hosted by The Student Health & Well-Being cluster in partnership with Recreation, The Good Life Festival is an event designed to celebrate healthy living, promote well-being resources, and engaging students in fun and exciting activities such as a climbing wall, photo booth, henna artists, face painters, aqua bubble rollers, land bubble rollers, Zumba, chair massage, healthy food, prizes, and giveaways... all for FREE! This event will be a night to remember, so come out and enjoy the #UCSDgoodlife!
